**Financial Health Indicators (Complete):**

**Green Flags (Genuine Sustainability)**:
- LTV:CAC >3:1 and stable or improving
- Positive contribution margin on unit basis
- CAC decreasing or flat as scale increases
- ARPU growing from genuine value addition
- Runway >12 months or clear path to profitability
- Diversified revenue (not single product/channel)

**Red Flags (Real Problems)**:
- Burn rate exceeding projections consistently
- CAC increasing faster than LTV
- Single revenue source dependency (concentration risk)
- Negative unit economics even at scale
- <6 months runway without clear funding path
- Missing revenue consistently (not just once)
- **Financial metrics improving but require creative interpretation**
