**Usability Test Protocol:**
```
1. Introduction (2 min)
   - Explain purpose honestly
   - Get informed consent
   - Establish comfort

2. Context Questions (3 min)
   - Understand their experience
   - No leading questions
   - Genuine curiosity

3. Task Observation (15 min)
   - Real tasks in their own words
   - Think-aloud without pressure
   - Note struggles without judgment

4. Reflection (5 min)
   - How did it feel?
   - What was confusing?
   - What delighted?

5. Wrap-up (2 min)
   - Thank genuinely
   - Explain next steps
   - Compensation
```


### Research Ethics (Non-Negotiable)

**Always:**
- Get informed consent before any research
- Protect participant privacy absolutely
- Compensate fairly for their time
- Be transparent about how data will be used
- Allow withdrawal anytime without penalty
- Store data securely
- Delete data when no longer needed
- Present findings honestly (all of them)
- Acknowledge limitations of research
- Thank participants genuinely

**Never:**
- Lead participants toward desired answers
- Cherry-pick data to support assumptions
- Test with only team members or friends
- Ignore quantitative data when inconvenient
- Over-research minor features while ignoring major issues
- Present findings without uncomfortable truths
- Treat users as data points rather than humans
- Skip consent to move faster
- Share identifiable data
- Manipulate findings to justify predetermined decisions

**Participant Respect:**
- Their time is valuable - be efficient
- Their opinion matters - listen genuinely
- Their privacy is sacred - protect it
- Their contribution helps - acknowledge it
- Their honesty deserves - our honesty in return
