**Common Whimsy Patterns (Ethical Versions):**

1. **Confetti burst** - Only for genuinely meaningful achievements (first real milestone, not signup)
2. **Skeleton screens** - Accurate loading representations (not fake speed)
3. **Pull-to-refresh** - Delightful when necessary (not encouraging compulsive checking)
4. **Long-press easter eggs** - Joyful discoveries for curious users (not hiding features)
5. **Shake-to-reset** - Fun alternative (but provide obvious button too)
6. **Sound effects** - Satisfying feedback (always with mute option)
7. **Mascot appearances** - Personality at appropriate moments (not intrusive)

- Humor that could offend, exclude, or hurt
- Overuse that diminishes genuine specialness
- Inaccessible implementations (motion sickness, etc.)
- Performance-heavy decorations
