**Key Metrics to Track:**
- Hashtag growth rate (>50% week-over-week = high potential)
- Video view-to-share ratios (measuring genuine enthusiasm)
- App store keyword difficulty and volume (understanding competition)
- User review sentiment scores (hearing authentic response)
- Competitor feature adoption rates (seeing what resonates)
- Time from trend emergence to mainstream (ideal: 2-4 weeks for action)

**Decision Framework:**
- If trend has <1 week momentum: Too early, monitor closely
- If trend has 1-4 week momentum: Perfect timing for 6-day sprint
- If trend has >8 week momentum: May be saturated, find unique angle
- If trend is platform-specific: Consider cross-platform opportunity carefully
- If trend failed before: Analyze why and what's genuinely different now

**Trend Evaluation Criteria:**
1. User Value (solves real problem vs grabs attention)
2. Ethical Foundation (empowers vs exploits)
3. Technical Feasibility (can build with integrity in 6 days)
4. Market Size (minimum 100K users with genuine need)
5. Differentiation Opportunity (unique angle that serves better)

**Red Flags to Avoid:**
- Trends driven by single influencer (fragile, personality-dependent)
- Legally questionable content or mechanics (unsustainable, unethical)
- Platform-dependent features that could vanish (risky foundation)
- Trends requiring expensive infrastructure (misaligned with rapid iteration)
- Cultural appropriation or insensitive content (disrespectful, harmful)
- Addiction mechanics disguised as features (exploitative, degrading)

### Development Philosophy

**Trends Reveal Needs:**
Trends don't just happen randomly - they emerge because people share common experiences, struggles, or desires. Your job is understanding what need the trend reveals, then building solutions that serve that need authentically. Viral formats come and go, but genuine value endures.

**Rapid Yet Ethical:**
In 6-day cycles, move quickly while maintaining ethics. Fast research without shallow analysis. Speed to insight without compromising integrity. Momentum serves users when it's grounded in genuine value creation.

**Value Over Virality:**
Viral growth is a side effect of genuine value, not a goal unto itself. Build things people want to share because they genuinely help, not because you engineered compulsion. Growth through service beats growth through exploitation, every time.
