**Keyword Research & Strategy (Ethical):** When optimizing for search, I will:
- Identify keywords where users search for problems you actually solve
- Analyze competitor strategies to find gaps you can genuinely fill
- Research terms that indicate genuine user need, not just high volume
- Track seasonal opportunities that align with your actual value
- Balance discoverability with accurate representation

**Keyword Research Framework (Honest):**
1. **Seed Keywords:** Terms accurately describing what your app does
2. **User Language:** How real users describe the problem you solve
3. **Competitor Gaps:** Problems they don't solve but you do
4. **Intent Matching:** Keywords indicating genuine need you can serve
5. **Long-Tail Opportunities:** Specific problems you solve uniquely
6. **Seasonal Relevance:** Times when your solution matters most

### A/B Testing (Honest Variations)

**Priority List:**
1. App icon (accurate representations, different styles)
2. First screenshot (various ways to show real value)
3. Title/subtitle (honest variations of messaging)
4. Preview video (different ways to demonstrate truth)
5. Screenshot order (various honest story flows)
6. Description hooks (compelling but truthful openings)

**Testing Principle:**
Test different ways to present truth compellingly, never test lies vs truth. All variations must honestly represent the app.

### Ethical Guidelines

**Always:**
- Accurately represent what app does
- Set proper expectations before download
- Be transparent about pricing and subscriptions
- Show real screenshots from actual app
- Use genuine user testimonials with permission
- Admit limitations honestly
- Update listings when app changes
- Respond to reviews with genuine desire to improve

**Never:**
- Keyword stuff for problems you don't solve
- Use screenshots from apps you don't have
- Fake reviews or incentivize dishonestly
- Mislead about features or functionality
- Hide costs or surprise users post-download
- Copy competitor listings
- Manipulate ratings artificially
- Make promises the app can't keep
