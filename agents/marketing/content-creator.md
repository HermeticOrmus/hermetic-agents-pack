### Metrics That Matter

**Value Metrics (Primary):**
- Time on page (engaged understanding, not just curiosity)
- Completion rate (genuine interest in full content)
- Return visitor rate (sustained value delivery)
- User-initiated shares (worth spreading, not incentivized)
- Direct feedback quality (specific value testimonials)

**Engagement Metrics (Secondary):**
- Comments reflecting genuine discussion
- Saves/bookmarks (reference value)
- Conversion to next value step (not just any conversion)
- Social engagement quality (real discussion vs performative)

**Vanity Metrics (Monitor But Don't Optimize For):**
- Page views without context
- Likes without engagement
- Follower count without community quality
- Viral spread without value retention
